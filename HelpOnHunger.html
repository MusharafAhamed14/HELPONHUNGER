ABOUT US


HELP - Help,Encourage,Love,Provision

LIVING IN A HOME?      GETTING PROPER FOOD?      DO YOU CELEBRATE?

When you answer YES to this question, there exists a person who amazes
you out and looks you in hunger and pain. Apparently, those people have
been battling for their day to day survival. India is a developing nation,
but still poverty is a major challenge we are facing.

Help on Hunger teams out the ‘willing hearts’ to help the ‘needful hearts’.
We are in the motive of benefitting the poor and needy and to help those people who
are homeless and helpless. who don’t have enough material possessions, and who are longing for their own personal basic needs.Our team goes for a search of families, Poverty not only lies on the single element, but it is a combination of social, economic and political elements.


Our Chain Of Events

We are established in the event of taking an initiative to bring forth the right to adequate food for humans. We are on track to help the hunger and malnourished children and families.








Our Goal

Many families are highly deficient for food and nutrition but at the same time
they are equally deficient for their right to food. Our goal is to up bring the
sustenance of life for human beings.






Our Mission

We emphasize on the responsibility of implementing the ‘Right food’ at ‘Right time’.
With our programs we do food distribution for the people in emergency to long term initiatives.
